About Direct2U Wine Aerator

Improved Wine Experience

Our wine aerator uses a patented three step process that instantly aerates your wine at a pace that is 300% faster than a traditional decanter No need for traditional decanting, the direct2u deluxe wine aerator pourer has a large aerating chamber and air intake system that infuses your wine with the optimal amount of oxygen. Increase the value of your wine by simply pouring yourself a glass.

The Direct2U Wine Aerator expands the aromatic profile and softens the tannins of your favorite wines. You’ll enjoy a deeper, more developed flavor as soon as you can pour a glass. No more waiting around on a decanter: you can enjoy your freshly aerated wine the moment the desire hits.

You don’t need to master any special skills to elevate your wine to new heights. Our foolproof design makes it effortless for anyone to enjoy the beautiful results of aeration. To use, simply place over the top of a glass and pour wine into the opening. The magic happens inside of the aerator, where our innovative design creates bubbles of air that mix with the wine as it flows.

Your wine will breathe with this fresh oxygen for just the optimal few seconds that it needs before flowing out from the bottom of the aerator and into your glass, ready to be enjoyed. Notice the improved aromas, heightened flavors, and velvety finish of a wine that has received the Direct2U treatment. You’ll never go back to your old ways of drinking wine.

Everyone can find a place for the Direct2U in their kitchen or bar. Wine enthusiasts and casual sippers alike can appreciate the expanded flavor profile of their favorite wines, young and old. Home entertainers who understand that time is of the essence will love its efficiency. Plus, the sleek, modern design makes the Direct2U an elegant lifestyle accessory that you’ll be proud to display in your home. You can finally experience the layers of vibrant flavors that have been lying dormant inside your favorite wines, waiting to be opened up. Unlock their full potential in just seconds with the Direct2U Wine Aerator.
